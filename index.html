Good to know

Completely. Build palettes, copy colours and export as much as you like, and the colours are yours to use however you want, commercial work included. If it earns a spot in your workflow, there's a tip jar — entirely optional.

Pick a base and the builder generates real harmonies (complementary, analogous, triadic, split-complementary and more), then balances lightness into five usable steps from ink to paper. Lock any swatch to pin it while the rest re-harmonise around it.

Every build ships a live WCAG contrast checker. Pair any two swatches and it shows the exact ratio with AA / AAA pass marks, so the colours you actually ship are legible, not just pretty.
